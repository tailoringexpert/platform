DRD-02.04 - Critical Items List (CIL)
Reference: ECSS-Q-ST-10C Space product assurance - Product assurance
ECSS-Q-ST-10-04C Space product assurance - Critical-item control
ECSS-Q-ST-30C Space product assurance - Dependability
ECSS-Q-ST-40C Space product assurance - Safety
ECSS-Q-ST-60C Space product assurance - Electrical, electronic and electromechanical (EEE) components
ECSS-Q-ST-70C Space product assurance - Material, mechanical parts and processes
ECSS-Q-ST-80C Space product assurance - Software product assurance
Purpose: To document and control critical items.
Content: This list shall include the following items used in flight equipmentt(according to ECSS-Q-ST-10-04C, Annex C):
  • safety critical items,
  • dependability critical items
  • single point failure items,
  • fracture critical items,
  • items with limited operating life, shelf life or tolerance to environmental exposure (e.g. vibration, acceleration, thermal, chemical, radiation) which warrants controlled surveillance under specified conditions,
  • critical PMPs and critical EEE parts,
  • software critical items,
  • items critical for integration
  • ITAR EEE component's
  • failures which are not readily detectable (no methods of generating a system advisory or providing a visible indicator) by the flight or ground crew during any mission phase.
Format In-house form (example is provided according to ECSS-Q-10-04C, Annex A):
  • Number: unique item identifier.
  • Critical item,
  • Risks associated,
  • Reference doc,
  • Criticality level,
  • Cause,
  • Control activities,
  • Due date
  • Status.

Example of Critical Item List:

No. Critical item Risk associated Reference doc. Criticality level Cause Control activities Due date Status


No. Unique item identifier.
Critical item Identified critical item (e.g. unit, subsystem, equipment, component, material, process, and function).
Risks associated Technical risk(s) associated with the critical item (refer to the associated entry in the Risk Register).
Reference doc. Reference document in which the item is identified as critical.
Criticality level In accordance with the critical item classification as defined in clause 5.2.3 of ECSS-Q-ST-10-04
Cause Description of the cause which makes this item critical.
Control activities (Refer to clause 5.2.4.2 Task 7 of ECSS-Q-ST-10-04). Planned activities to reduce or control the risk and statement of verification of the control implementation (e.g. design and operational requirements, test, inspection and failure history).
Due date Expected completion date of activities.
Status Status of action: Open / Closed (with ref. to close-out docs.)
