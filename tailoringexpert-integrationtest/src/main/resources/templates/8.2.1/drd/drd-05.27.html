DRD-05.27 - Payload Hazard Report (ISS_OE_851)
Reference: ECSS-Q-ST-40-02C Space product assurance - Hazard analysis
SSP-51700 Baseline Payload Safety Policy and Requirements for the International Space Station
NSTS/ISS 13830 Payload Safety Review and Data Submittal Requirements
Purpose: Hazard Reports (HR) shall document the specific potential hazard identified by analysis.
One HR (specific/ unique) shall be prepared for each hazard identified.
Details see NSTS 13830, section 5.4 and 6.
Content: The report shall show potential causes, identifying controls, relate results of verification and provide traceability.
The data shall be brief and concise with enough back up data supplied so that each report can be a "stand-alone" document.
Summarize referenced detailed analysis, tests, and test results in order to provide enough facts to allow the safety review team to make a value judgment.
