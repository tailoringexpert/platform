DRD-05.13 - Series and Reflown Equipment Safety Assessment Reporting Sheet
Reference: ISS_OE_622 Series and Reflown Equipment Safety Assessment Reporting Sheet
Demonstration of Compliance with SSP 30599, Para. 7.0
NSTS/ISS 13830 Payload Safety Review and Data Submittal Requirements
Purpose: Reflown HW/Equipment is defined as payloads or elements of payloads that have flown on the ISS and are manifested for flight.  Series flight HW is defined as payloads or elements of payloads that are of the same or similar design as previously flown HW.The PO is responsible for the safety of the total payload/GSE, including the series/reflown elements and associated interfaces.
