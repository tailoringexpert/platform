DRD-05.06 - ATV Flight Safety Certificate (Form 879)
Reference: ESA-ATV-PR-13830 ATV Pressurised Payload/Cargo Safety Certification Process
Purpose: This DRD provides the template to establish the ATV Flight Safety Certificate.
This DRD provides the minimum  inputs to the ATV Cargo integrator in order to perform the integrated safety analysis.
Content: See ESA-ATV-PR-13830
Format: Form 879 in annex
