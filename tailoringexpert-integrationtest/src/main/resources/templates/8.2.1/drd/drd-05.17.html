NSTS/ISS 13830 Payload Safety Review and Data Submittal Requirements
Purpose: Document the payload organization's (PO's) safety assessment in a manner that reflects how the payload design demonstrates compliance with the safety requirements. The HRs are used as a method to systematically assess compliance with the safety requirements. All flight HRs are contained in the Flight Safety Data Package (FSDP); all ground hazards are contained in the Ground Safety Data Package (GSDP).
Hazards specified on the ISS_OE_1298 are defined as "standard". Hazards that are not reflected in the standard HR are defined as "unique".
Content: Dependent on the complexity, technical maturity, and hazard potential for manned missions standard HRs shall be prepared on ISS_OE_1298 (see DRD-5.18) or unique HRs on ISS_OE_851 (see DRD-5.19) or equivalent form that contains all information required on the ISS forms. For unmanned missions see DRD-5-26.
