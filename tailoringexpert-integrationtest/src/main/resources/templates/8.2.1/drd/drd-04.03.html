DRD-04.03 - Reliability Prediction Report
Reference: MIL-HDBK-217 Reliability Prediction of Electronic Equipment
MIL-STD-756 Reliability Modelling and Prediction
MIL-HDBK-338 Electronic Reliability Design Handbook
ECSS-Q-ST-30C Space product assurance - Dependability
ECSS-Q-HB-30-08 Components reliability data sources and their use
Purpose: To enable a transparent documentation of reliability prediction results.
Content: Reliability prediction reports shall contain the following information:
  • scope,
  • applicable documents,
  • functional diagram and description indicating design status,
  • mission objectives describing primary and secondary functions to be performed by system/equipment in order to achieve the mission success,
  • duty cycles and/or accumulated total operating time during all phases,
  • reliability block diagram,
  • list of failure rates and the associated sources,
  • identification and description of the used mathematical model,
  • parts count matrix,
  • comparison between calculated and allocated values,
  • conclusions and recommendations.
Format: The in-house format may be used.
