DRD-05.16 - Fault Tree Analysis (FTA)
Reference: ECSS-Q-ST-30-11C Space product assurance - Derating - EEE components
ECSS-Q-ST-40C Space product assurance - Safety
ECSS-Q-ST-30C pace product assurance - Dependability
ECSS-Q-ST-40-12C Space product assurance - Fault Tree Analysis - Adoption notice ECSS/IEC 61025
Purpose: The analysis has to be performed on SPFs and hazardous items with special emphasis on analogue circuitry including power supplies, RF-circuitry, critical interfaces e.g. ECL devices and optocouplers and CMOS as well as power-MOS devices which are susceptible to ionizing radiation. The parameter degradation given in ECSS-Q-60-11 and ECSS-Q-30-11 shall be applied. Any adaptation of these data to the actual mission duration or the use of other data sources is subject for approval by DLR. Radiation dose values shall be taken from separate radiation analysis which takes into account the internal radiation shielding of the analysed equipment. In case of use of radiation non-resistant components, data on parameter degradation caused by radiation shall be provided by representative sample tests. At least twice the maximum degradation resulting from these tests shall be considered for worst case analysis. Non-compliances with the specified functional requirements which cannot be settled are subject to a Request for Waiver.
Content:
Format The parameter deviation data shall be presented in tabular form similar to the derating analysis. The in-house format containing sufficient information on the assumptions, method and results shall be used.
