DRD-05.12 - ISS Payload Safety Requirements Application Matrix
Reference: SSP-51700 baseline Payload Safety Policy and Requirements for the International Space Station
Purpose: The matrix shall provide a systematic approach for identifying those SSP-51700 requirements applicable to a particular payload element. The purpose of the matrix is to provide objective evidence that the PED has considered every requirement of the SSP-51700 in assessing the safety of his proposed design.
