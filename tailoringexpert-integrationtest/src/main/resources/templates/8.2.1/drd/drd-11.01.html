DRD-11.01 - Space Debris Mitigation Declaration
Reference: DLR-RF-PS-001 Product Assurance & Safety Requirements for DLR-Space Projects
Purpose: The objective of the Space Debris Mitigation Declaration shall be to provide a brief summary of all design measures and operational procedures applicable to limit the creation of orbital debris and, therefore, to limit the risk to other current and future space missions and to limit the risk to human life due to generated orbital debris.
