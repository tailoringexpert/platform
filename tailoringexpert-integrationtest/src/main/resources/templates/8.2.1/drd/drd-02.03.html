DRD-02.03 - Product Assurance Reporting
Reference: ECSS-Q-ST-10C Space product assurance - Product assurance management
Purpose: To demonstrate progress in all areas of Product Assurance (PA) tasks and to highlight problem areas for the attention of DLR-RF.
Content: Depending on the phase of the programme and the tasks currently being performed the report shall include:
  • Progress and accomplishment of each major product assurance task including resolved and new problems,
  • future planning of major activities and events,
  • Status of:
    1. PA reviews (technical reviews, preparation status and conclusions),
    2. PA documentation,
    3. Audits and MIPs,
    4. requests for Waiver, Deviation and Approval
    5. Non conformances (minor and major),
    6. Critical items (including mitigation action plan status),
    7. Qualification,
    8. EEE component,
    9. Material and processes,
    10. Alerts and Lessons Learned
The PA progress report may be part of the project progress report.

The general contents of the project progress report are detailed in ECSS-M-ST-10 Annex E.

Format: In-house
