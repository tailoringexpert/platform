DRD-04.01 - Reliability Prediction (Parts Count Technique)
Reference: MIL-HDBK-217 Reliability Prediction of Electronic Equipment
MIL-STD-756 Reliability Modelling and Prediction
MIL-HDBK-338 Electronic Reliability Design Handbook
NPRD-91 Non-electronic Parts Reliability Data 1991
Purpose: To provide the quantitative baseline needed to assess progress in reliability engineering. The Parts Count Method shall be used during the proposal phase as well as in the early project phases.
Content: Equipment reliability shall be calculated as a function of the number, type and quality level of the individual parts within the equipment.
Format: The in-house format to be used shall be similar to the annexed format and shall contain at least the same information.
