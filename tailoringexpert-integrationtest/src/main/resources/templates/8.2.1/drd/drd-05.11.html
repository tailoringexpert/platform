DRD-05.11 - EP5 Battery Design Evaluation Form (ISS_EP_03)
Reference: ISS_EP_03 EP5 Battery Design Evaluation Form
NSTS/ISS 13830C Payload Safety Review and Data Submittal Requirements
JSC-20793B Crewed space vehicle battery safety requirements
Purpose: The purpose of this form is to support the payload safety review process in order to assist the Payload Organizations (PO's) in documenting compliance with the payload requirements documents. Sufficient supporting data must be attached to the EP-form to demonstrate complete compliance with requirements.

The objective of the safety review process is to review the payload, Ground Support Equipment (GSE), and operations for adequate safety implementation. The mission success and any scientific objectives of the payload are the responsibility of the PO and are beyond the scope of this document and process.
Content: Data submittal requirements included herein applies to hardware/battery being submitted to both the Payload Safety Review Panel (PSRP) and the Ground Safety Review Panel (GSRP) unless specified otherwise. The PSRP and the GSRP reserve the right to request additional data as deemed necessary to support safety documentation.

The form shall be submitted:
  • as part of the SDP or
  • submitted to the PSRP Executive Secretary/GSRP Chairman.
Format: The ISS_EP_03 will be used as the standard form for the support.
