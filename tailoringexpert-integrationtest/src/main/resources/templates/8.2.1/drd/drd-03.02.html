DRD-3.02 - Non-Conformance Report Status List
Reference: ECSS-Q-ST-20C Space product assurance - Quality assurance
ECSS-Q-ST-10-09C Space Product Assurance - Nonconformance control system
Purpose: A Non-Conformance Report Status List (NCR Status List) shall be maintained, providing a complete representation of all non-conformances occurring in the frame of a business agreement, for each product, at any time.
The NCR Status List shall cover major and minor NCRs and shall be part of the periodic PA Status Report (DRD-2.03) to the customer, unless otherwise required by the business agreement.
Also the NCTS status list shall be accepted.
Content: For each NCR, at least the following information shall be included:
  • Supplier of the nonconforming item
  • NCR unique identification,
  • NC item identification,
  • short description of the NC,
  • date of last NRB,
  • disposition,
  • implementation status of the disposition,
  • reference to RFW (if applicable),
  • open/closed status.
Format: In-house
