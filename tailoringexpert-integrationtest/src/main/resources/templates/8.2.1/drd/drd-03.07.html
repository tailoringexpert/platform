DRD-03.07 - Mandatory Inspection Point Report (MIP-Report)
Reference: N/A
Purpose: To check and document compliance and acceptability of the product on the basis of the production documentation.
Content: The MIP Report shall be established to document the results of any MIP or safety related inspection.
The MIP Report shall be submitted to the customer after each event.
Format: In-house
