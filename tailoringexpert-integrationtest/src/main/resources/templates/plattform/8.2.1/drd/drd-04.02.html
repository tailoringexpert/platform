DRD-04.02 - Reliability Prediction (Parts Stress Technique)
Reference: MIL-HDBK-217 Reliability Prediction of Electronic Equipment
MIL-STD-756 Reliability Modelling and Prediction
MIL-HDBK-338 Electronic Reliability Design Handbook
Purpose: To provide the quantitative baseline needed to assess progress in reliability engineering. The part stress analysis shall be used during the later project phase.
Content: The equipment failure rate shall be determined as a sum of all individual part failure rates, considering part types, quality level, operational stress and derating characteristics.
Format: The in-house format to be used shall be similar to the annexed format and shall contain at least the same information.
