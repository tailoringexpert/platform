DRD-3.09 - Übernahme/Übergabe-Testprotokoll (ÜÜT)
Reference: N/A
Purpose: This shall be applicable only for missions using Russian flights.
The Übernahme-/Übergabeprüfungen shall contain:
  • Qualification tests(QT),
  • Acceptance tests(AT).
Content: For each instrument the details and extend of the QT and AT have to be agreed with the Russian responsibles. Qualification tests cover also tests of functionality of the instruments and verification of conformity with the requirements defined in the system specification.
Acceptance tests are performed on flight models and are executed in two steps:
  1. Acceptance tests at the premises of the supplier (ÜÜT-1)
  2. Acceptance tests at the premises of the recipient (ÜÜT-2).
The results shall be documented in the ÜÜT
