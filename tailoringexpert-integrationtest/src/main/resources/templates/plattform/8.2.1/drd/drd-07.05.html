DRD-07.05 - Ionizing Radiation Source Data Sheet (JSC Form 44)
Reference: JSC Form 44 Ionizing Radiation Source Data Sheet Space Flight Hardware and Applications
NSTS/ISS 13830 Payload Safety Review and Data Submittal Requirements for Payloads Using the Space Shuttle & International Space Station
KHB 1860.1 KSC Ionizing Radiation Protection Program
SSP 30599 Safety Review Process, International Space Station Alpha Program
Purpose: The purpose of this document/form is to support the payload safety review process in order to assist the Payload Organizations (PO's) in documenting compliance with the payload requirements documents. Sufficient supporting data must be attached to the HRs (see DRD-5.19) to complete the presentation of hazard control and to demonstrate complete compliance with requirements. The objective of the safety review process is to review the payload, Ground Support Equipment (GSE), and operations for adequate safety implementation. The mission success and any scientific objectives of the payload are the responsibility of the PO and are beyond the scope of this document and process.
Content: Data submittal requirements included herein apply to HW being submitted to both the Payload Safety Review Panel (PSRP) and the Ground Safety Review Panel (GSRP) unless specified otherwise. The PSRP and the GSRP reserve the right to request additional data as deemed necessary to support safety documentation (supporting technical SDA data submittals).

Each ISSA HR should stand alone. Data required to understand the hazard should be attached to the report. The support data shall be prepared where applicable and also submitted for review (phase HRs).

Attached supporting data are:
  • JSC Form 44 describing all uses of radioactive materials or radiation generators;
  • Radioactive Payloads Working Group approval memorandum for JSC Form 44.
The form shall be submitted:
  • attached to the HR,
  • as part of the SDP or
  • submitted to the PSRP Executive Secretary/GSRP Chairman.
The JSC Form 44 will be used as the standard form for the support.
Format:
  • PSRP: New/updated JSC Form 44.
    GSRP: Forms in accordance with KHB 1860.1 (if required).
  • Initially submitted at the different phases (I to III).
