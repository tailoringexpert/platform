DRD-08.01 - Software Product Assurance Plan (SPAP)
Reference: ECSS-E-ST-40C Space engineering - Software
ECSS-Q-ST-80C Space product assurance - Software product assurance
Purpose: The Software Product Assurance Plan describes the approach to the implementation of the software product assurance programme for a software product. The Software Product Assurance Plan provides for the definition of organizational aspects and the management approach to the implementation of the software product assurance tasks.
Content:

Title

The Software Product Assurance Plan to be created on this DRD shall be titled "[Insert a descriptive modifier] Software Product Assurance Plan". The descriptive modifier shall be selected to clearly identify the applicable product and level specification.

Title page

The title page shall identify the project document identification number, title of the document, date of release and release authority.

Contents list

The content list shall identify the title and location of every clause and major subclause, figure, table and annex contained in the Software Product Assurance Plan.

Foreword

A foreword shall be included which describes as many of the following items as appropriate:
  • identification of which organizational entities prepared the document;
  • information regarding the approval of the document;
  • identification of other organizations that contributed to the preparation of the document;
  • a statement of effectivity identifying which other documents are cancelled and replaced in whole or in part;
  • a statement of significant technical differences between this document and any previous document;
  • the relationship of the document to other standards or documents.

Introduction

An introduction may be included to provide specific information or commentary about the technical contents of the Software Product Assurance Plan.

Scope and applicability

This clause shall describe the scope, applicability and purpose of the document.

1.1 Scope

This subclause shall contain a statement to define the goal of the Software Product Assurance Plan.

1.2 Applicability

  1. This subclause shall contain a statement to define the use of the Software Product Assurance Plan.
  2. This subclause shall also:
  • identify the software products under development;
  • summarize the software product assurance programme;
  • specify the documentation driving the software product assurance programme;
  • define the relationship to other plans.
     

1.3 Document organization

This subclause shall describe what the rest of the document contains and explain how the rest of the document is organized.

4 Software product assurance process implementation

This clause shall contain the following subclauses:

4.1 Organization

  1. The SPAP shall describe the organization of software product assurance activities, including responsibility, authority and the interrelation of personnel who manage, perform and verify work affecting software quality.
  2. Topics that shall be included are:
  • organizational structure;
  • interfaces of each organisation, either external or internal, involved in the project;
  • relationship to the system level product assurance and safety;
  • independence of the software product assurance function;
  • delegation of software product assurance tasks to a lower level supplier, if any.

4.2 Responsibilities

This subclause shall describe the responsibilities of the software product assurance function.

4.3 Planning

This subclause shall describe the logical schedule of the planned software product assurance activities, in particular all software product assurance activities to be performed at the identified project milestones.

4.4 Assurance and control activities

This subclause shall describe the assurance and control activities to be performed by software product assurance to ensure adherence to the project plans, standards and procedures.

4.5 Reporting

This subclause shall describe the reporting to be performed by software product assurance.

4.6 Risk management

This subclause shall describe the contribution of the software product assurance function to the project risk management.

4.7 Subcontractor selection and control

This subclause shall describe the contribution of the software product assurance function to the subcontractor selection and control.

4.8 Resources

  1. This subclause shall describe the resources needed to perform the software product assurance function.
  2. This description shall include human resources and skills, hardware and software tools.

5 Software development process assurance

This clause shall contain the following subclauses:

5.1 Software development life cycle

  1. This subclause shall refer to the software development cycle description in the software development plan.
  2. If not covered in the software development plan, the life cycle shall be described in this subclause.

5.2 Software documentation and configuration management

This subclause shall describe the contribution of the software product assurance function to the proper implementation of documentation and configuration management.
The nonconformance control system shall be described or referenced. The point in the software life cycle from which the nonconformance procedures apply shall be specified.
This subclause shall identify method and tool to protect the supplied software, a checksum-type key calculation for the delivered operational software, and a labeling method for the delivered media.

5.3 Software dependability and safety

This subclause shall describe the handling of critical software including the analysis to be performed and the standards applicable for critical software.

5.4 Procedures and standards

  1. This subclause shall describe or identify by reference all procedures and standards applicable to the development of the software in the project.
  2. The software product assurance measures to ensure adherence to the project procedures and standards shall be described. The aspects to be covered by standards and procedures are:
  • project management;
  • risk management;
  • configuration and documentation management;
  • verification and validation;
  • requirements engineering;
  • design;
  • coding;
  • metrication;
  • non conformance control;
  • audits;
  • alerts;
  • procurement;
  • reuse of existing software;
  • use of methods and tools;
  • numerical accuracy;
  • delivery, installation and acceptance;
  • operations;
  • maintenance;
  • device programming and marking.

6 Software product quality assurance

  1. This clause shall describe the approach taken to ensure the quality of the software product.
  2. The description of this approach shall specifically include the:
  • specification of the product metrics, their target values and the means to collect them;
  • analyses to be performed on the collected metrics;
  • way the results are fed back to the development team;
  • quality requirements.
