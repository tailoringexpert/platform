DRD-08.08 - Installation Plan
Reference: N/A
Purpose: The (Software) Installation Plan describes the procedures for installing and configuring the software application in the production environments, including preparations and conversion from existing systems.
Content: The content of the installation Plan is in detail:

Title

The document to be created based on this DRD shall be titled "[insert a descriptive modifier] Software Installation Plan".
The descriptive modifier shall be selected to clearly identify the applicable product and level specification.

Title page

The title page for this document shall identify the project document identification number, title of the document, date of release and release authority.

Contents list

The contents list shall identify the title and location of every clause and major subclause, figure, table and annex contained in the document.

Foreword

A foreword shall be included in the document which describes as many of the following items:
  • identification of which organizational entity prepared the document,
  • information regarding the approval of the document,
  • identification of other organizations that contributed to the preparation of the document,
  • a statement of effectivity identifying which other documents are cancelled and replaced in whole or in part,
  • a statement of significant technical differences between this document and any previous document,
  • the relationship of the document to other standards or documents.

Introduction

An introduction may be included to provide specific information or commentary about the technical contents.

1. Scope and applicability

This clause shall describe the scope, applicability, and purpose of the (Software) Installation Plan document.

1.1 Scope

  1. This subclause shall contain a statement to define the goal of the (Software) Installation Plan.
  2. This subclause shall:
    • identify the software products to be produced by name and allocated CI number and
    • describe the main capabilities provided by the software product.

1.2 Purpose

This subclause shall contain a statement to define the use of the (Software) Installation Plan.

1.3 Document organization

This subclause shall describe what the rest of the document contains and shall explain how the rest of the document is organized.

This clause shall contain the following subclauses.

  1. This subclause shall list any applicable project dictionary or glossary, and all unusual terms or terms with a meaning specific to this (Software) Installation Plan, with the definition for each term.

3.2 Abbreviated terms

4. Installation overview

This clause shall provide a general description of the installation process to provide a frame of reference.

4.1 Installation process

This subclause shall include a list of sites for software installation, the schedule dates, the method of installation, the operation, and the maintenance.

4.2 Environmental considerations

This subclause shall summarize:
  • the physical environment of the target system,
  • the hardware environment in the target system,
  • the operating environment in the target system.

4.3 Support materials

This subclause shall list the type, source, and quantity of support materials needed for the installation.

4.4 Training

This subclause shall describe the supplier's plans for training personnel who will operate and/or use the software installed at customer sites.

4.5 Constraints

  1. This subclause shall describe any items that will limit the options for installation the software.
  2. It should provide background information and seek to justify the constraints.

5. Installation Plan

This clause describes all activities for installation of software.

5.x (Site name)

This subclause shall identify a site or set of sites and shall be divided into the following subclauses to discuss those sites. Multiple sites may be discussed together when the information for those sites is generally the same.

5.x.1 Schedule

This subclause shall present a schedule of tasks to be accomplished during installation. It shall depict the tasks in chronological order with beginning and ending dates of each task and supporting narrative as necessary.

5.x.2 Software inventory

This subclause shall provide an inventory of the software needed to support the installation. The software shall be identified by name, identification number, version number, release number, configuration, and security classification, as applicable. This paragraph shall indicate whether the software is expected to be on site or will be delivered for the installation and shall identify any software to be used only to facilitate the installation process.

5.x.3 Facilities

This subclause shall detail the physical facilities and accommodations needed during the installation period. This description shall include the following, as applicable:
  1. Classroom, work space, and training aids needed, specifying hours per day, number of days, and shifts
  2. Hardware that must be operational and available
  3. Transportation and lodging for the installation team.

5.x.4 Installation team

This subclause shall describe the composition of the installation team with the number, type, and skill level of the personnel needed during the installation period, including the need for multishift operation. Each team member's tasks shall be defined.

5.x.5 Installation procedures

This subclause shall provide step-by-step procedures for accomplishing the installation. References may be made to other documents, such as software user manual. Safety precautions, marked by WARNING or CAUTION, shall be included where applicable. The procedures shall include the following, as applicable:
  1. Entry and exit criteria
  2. Installing the software
  3. Test of installed software
  4. Initializing databases and other software with site-specific data
  5. Conversion from the current system, possibly involving running in parallel
  6. Dry run of the procedures in software user manual
  7. Backup procedure.

5.x.6 Data update procedures

This subclause shall present the data update procedures to be followed during the installation period. When the data update procedures are the same as normal updating or processing procedures, reference may be made to other documents, such as software user manual.

6. Traceability

  1. This subclause shall report the traceability matrices from the upper level specification requirements to the requirements contained in clause 5 and from the requirements contained in clause 5 to the upper level applicable specification (forward and backward traceability tables).
  2. In case this information is separately provided in the DJF, reference to this documentation shall be clearly stated.
