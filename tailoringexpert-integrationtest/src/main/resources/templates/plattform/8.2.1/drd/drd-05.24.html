DRD-05.24 - Safety Verification Tracking Log (SVTL)
Reference: SSP-51700
Baseline		 Payload Safety Policy and Requirements for the International Space Station
NSTS/ISS 13830 Payloads Safety Review and Data Submittal Requirements for Payloads Using the Space Shuttle/ International Space Station
ISS_OE_764 Safety Verification Tracking LOG (SVTL)
Purpose: All flight and ground safety verifications that are open at time of the phase III SDP submittal must be tracked on the Safety Verification Tracking Log (SVTL). Details see NSTS 13830, section 5.7, and 6.5.
Content: All flight safety verifications that are still incomplete at phase III, must be "closed" on the HR and transferred to the flight safety VTL for further tracking. This log will allow the Payload Safety Review Panel (PSRP) to sign the HRs, indicating completion of the safety analysis, but with the understanding that approval for flight will be withheld until all verification activity is completed. Similarly, all open ground verifications must be listed on the ground safety VTL. This log will allow the Ground Safety Review Panel (GSRP) to sign the HRs, indicating completion of the safety analysis. Open ground verifications and open flight verifications that have been identified as a constraint against payload processing must be closed before the applicable ground operation can be performed.
Format For payloads using the ISS the format shall be the annexed ISS_OE_764.
