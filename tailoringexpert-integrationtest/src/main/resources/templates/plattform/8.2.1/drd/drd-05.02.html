DRD-05.02 - Instrument Safety Certification
Reference:
Purpose: The supplier shall certify to the customer (DLR-RFA) and to the Launch Authority that the HW/SW meets the imposed Safety Requirements.
The certification process shall be completed before delivery.
The certificate shall be provide to the customer after completion of all required verifications for the flight HW/SW.
Content: The certification shall reflect the "as built" configuration.
The certification shall include a statement that open verifications shall be closed and do not affect further safe processing at third party premises. Closure process for open actions will be defined in agreement with the Launcher Authority.
It shall be the responsibility of the supplier to provide to the certification authority all safety related information that is required to enable the statement of safety compliance to be accepted and understood.
After signature of the certificate by the manufacturer and DLR-RFA, the document shall be submitted to the Russian authority for acceptance.
