DRD-03.06 - Mandatory Inspection Point Plan (MIP-Plan)
Reference: ECSS-Q-ST-20C Space product assurance - Quality assurance
ECSS-Q-ST-70C Space product assurance - Materials, mechanical parts and processes
Purpose: The purpose of this plan shall be to establish an inspection plan for inspections and tests at the points of manufacturing, assembly and integration, where maximum assurance for correct processing and prevention of unrecoverable or costly non-conformances can be obtained.

All pre-flight safety inspections shall be assessed for inclusion in the plan.
Content: The plan shall define the MIPs. The plan shall identify those MIPs where DLR-RF shall be notified.
Also, the plan shall include:
  • inspection point description,
  • subsystem,
  • verification requirement (final inspection, functional performance etc.),
  • acceptance requirement (e.g. inspection of documentation),
  • report number,
  • location of inspection,
  • model (EM, QM, FM, etc.).
Format: In-house
