Tailoring Catalogue – In General

The tailoring catalogue is prepared by a working group composed by competent representatives from important prime space.
The tailoring levels applied in this requirement catalogue are defined in the table below.

NOTE:
Due dates are further clarified by agreements:
  • With respect to milestones (e.g. due events listed in an agreement) the DLR-RFM provides review procedures including specific document delivery lists which might be amended by mission specific needs, as agreed between all parties, and time line for review data package provision, distribution of review item discrepancies (RID), RID answering as well as colloquium.
  • With respect to reviews which are required by management, engineering and PA requirements but are no agreed milestones, the supplier is responsible for issuing a review procedure and running the review process. Information on such reviews and participation of DLR-RFM has to be agreed between the parties.
    NOTE:
    Conduct of reviews is a project management task.
